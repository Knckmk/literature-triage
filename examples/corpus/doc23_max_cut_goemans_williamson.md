# Max-cut and the Goemans-Williamson SDP
Goemans and Williamson gave a 0.878-approximation for max-cut via semidefinite programming relaxation.
The hyperplane rounding analysis is a milestone in approximation theory.
We discuss its connections to UGC-hardness and the wider SDP rounding agenda.
While not directly about TSP, the techniques inform many graph approximation arguments.
