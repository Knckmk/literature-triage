# Subcubic 2-edge-connected spanning subgraph approximation
We study the minimum-size 2-edge-connected spanning subgraph problem in subcubic graphs.
A polynomial-time approximation with ratio strictly better than 5/4 is obtained using ear decompositions.
The analysis avoids LP-based bounds and instead leverages local exchange arguments on short ears.
Lower bound constructions are discussed for the integrality gap of the natural LP relaxation.
