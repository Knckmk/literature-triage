# Christofides-style 3/2 approximation for metric TSP
The Christofides algorithm achieves a 3/2 approximation ratio for the metric Traveling Salesman Problem.
The construction combines a minimum spanning tree with a minimum weight perfect matching on odd-degree vertices.
This parity correction step is the source of the approximation guarantee and remains a baseline in modern analyses.
We outline the algorithm, the matching subroutine, and the integrality gap implications.
