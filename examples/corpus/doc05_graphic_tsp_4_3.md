# Towards a 4/3 approximation for graphic TSP
We survey progress on the long-standing 4/3 conjecture for graphic TSP.
Subcubic graphs and 2-edge-connected instances admit improved bounds via ear decompositions and structural arguments.
Parity correction is achieved without resorting to a generic matching, instead exploiting graph structure.
Recent rounding schemes connect LP relaxations to combinatorial fixes for odd-degree vertices.
